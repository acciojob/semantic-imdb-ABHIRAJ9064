The first of two cinematic parts, the film follows Sivudu, an adventurous young man who helps his love Avantika rescue Devasena, the former queen of Mahishmati

Prabhas, born Venkata Satyanarayana Prabhas Raju Uppalapati, is a popular Telugu film actor and nephew of veteran Telugu actor Krishnam Raju. He made his film debut with the 2002 drama film, Eshwar. His works include Varsham (2004), Chatrapathi (2005), Chakram (2005), Billa (2009), Mr. Perfect (2011), and Mirchi (2013). In 2014, he made his Bollywood debut with Action Jackson. In 2015, he played a titular role in the epic Baahubali: The Beginning. The sequel of this movie is set to release in 2017.
